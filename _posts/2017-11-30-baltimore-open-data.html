--- layout: post title: Analysis of Baltimore Policing Open Data excerpt: A two-part article with visualization and analysis of Baltimore's open data on arrests and calls for service. ---

The City of Baltimore has, for some time now, made its "Part I" Arrest and Calls-for-Service data available on the city's Open Data Portal. I thought it would be fun and interesting to dig into these datasets and see what I could produce in the way of visualizations and analyses. The result is a two-part article:

  • Part I: Visualization of Arrests in Baltimore
  • Part II: Arrests and Calls for Service in Baltimore

Thanks to the City of Baltimore for its commitment to open data, and thanks to several friends and colleagues who provided valuable feedback on early drafts. Enjoy!
